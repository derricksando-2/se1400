Website Summary

Geolocation

  • Primary Audience Location: Homeowners in middle-class and upper-middle-class neighborhoods, likely in suburban or semi-urban areas where home remodeling is a common need.

Age

  • Primary Age Range: 30-60 years old. Homeowners in this age range are typically more established in their careers and financially stable, with a desire to invest in their homes for long-term comfort and value.

Gender

  • Primary Gender: Both male and female, as home remodeling decisions are often shared by both partners in a household, although males may dominate decision-making in construction-related areas.

Other

  • Family-Oriented: The majority of the audience are families looking for a personalized, comfortable home environment. Income Level: Middle-class to upper-middle-class homeowners, likely in the income range that supports discretionary spending on home renovations. Homeownership Status: Homeowners who have settled in their homes and are looking to improve or remodel specific areas, such as kitchens and bathrooms.
